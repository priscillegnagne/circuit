Welcome to the “Gourmet Tour of Mont-Laurier” web page. This page presents three points of interest in the city of Mont-Laurier. For those who do not know it, this city is located in the Hautes-Laurentides, about 2h30-3h drive from Montreal and 2h-2h30 drive from Ottawa. Nestled in a tourist region and close to resorts, Mont-Laurier is a city of more than 14,000 inhabitants, welcoming and where it’s good to live. Come discover or rediscover the city of Mont-Laurier and its local products. Nothing like a gourmet tour to get to know a new place. Do you know of any other points of interest to visit in Mont-Laurier? Do not hesitate to suggest them by filling out the form.
